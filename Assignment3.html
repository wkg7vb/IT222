Of the four images, It is very difficult to distinguish between the JPEG, PNG and BMP files as far as quality goes, while the GIF was clearly compressed and lacks the same color range.
The JPEG has the smallest size, followed by the GIF, then the PNG, and the BMP has the largest.
I think that JPEG files would be best for webpages because the JPEG is the smallest file with indistinguishable quality from the larger files types.
