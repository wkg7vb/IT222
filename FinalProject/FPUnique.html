Kansas City Concert Venues

The T-Mobile Center


The T-Mobile Center is an arena in downtown Kansas City Missouri. Located on the east side of Power and Light District. This Arena was open in 2007 in Elton John was the first person to ever do a live event there. with over 19,000 feet and 72 levels the T-Mobile Center is a place where you would want to be for a concert.

Address: 1407 Grand Blvd, Kansas City, MO 64106

Prices: depends on the type of concert and seats.

Starlight Theatres


The Starlight theater in Kansas City Missouri holds about around 8,000 seats and it's an outdoor theater that represents Broadway shows and or concerts. This theater is a self-producing outdoor theater and it is one of the two major remaining outdoor theaters in the U.S.A.

Address: 4600 Starlight Rd, Kansas City, MO 64132

Prices: Depends on seat and location

Uptown Theatre


Uptown Theater is the most historical theater located in Kansas City Missouri. The building was listed on the register of historic places in 1979. This theater can be used for concerts and plays.

Address: 3700 Broadway Blvd, Kansas City, MO 64111

Price: depends on concert and seating.
