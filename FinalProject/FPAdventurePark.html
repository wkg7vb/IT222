Kansas City Adventure Parks

Worlds of Fun


Worlds of Fun is an entertainment park located in Kansas City, Missouri. Believe it or not, Worlds of Fun is the largest amusement park and the biggest water park located in the Midwest. The park's inspiration is from Jules Verne’s book, Around the World in Eighty Days. A quest to travel around the world in approximately eighty days. Opened in May 26, 1973 about 49 almost hitting 50 years, Worlds of Fun has thriving with the different types adventures for families everywhere. Interestingly enough, Worlds of Fun is founded by former NFL Player, Lamar Hunt. People would want to visit Worlds of Fun because it is one of the biggest amusement parks in the midwest.

Address: 4545 Worlds of Fun Ave, Kansas City, MO 64161

Price ranges for meals/tickets for Worlds of Fun: Price rages vary from daily admissions to season passes. The average amount for a daily admission ticket would be around $34.99 and for season passes range from 99 dollars for a gold pass to $230 for a platinum pass. Depending on what you buy, there is an affordable price for everyone. For food, prices vary from each corner shop you attend. Worlds of Fun offers you with “All Day Dining” which is $29.99 and a “Single Meal Deal” which is $14.99, and $99 for a whole seasons worth of food.

Urban Air Trampoline and Adventure Park


An indoor amusement park that is perfect for all ages. Urban Air and Adventure park is more than a trampoline park. Their mission is to connect and bring together families by a simple activity. The number one thing Urban Air Trampoline and Adventure Park focuses on is safety for both kids and parents. Targeted for families, Urban air can be for anyone. The park features different attractions which is perfect for all ages. A perfect place for birthday parties and special events to ensure family fun.

Address: 8554 Maurer Road, Lenexa, Kansas 66219

Price ranges for tickets and meals: Urban Air has plenty of options for families. They offer tickets ranging from ages from 5 and under to a platinum ticket. The prices range from $11.99 - $33.99. Only drinks are available at Urban air, but there are plenty of food options near Urban Air.
