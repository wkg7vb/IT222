Conclusion

For files that are the same size, at 454 KB, they are 44kHz, 8 bit and 22kHz, 16 bit. I think that they sound pretty much indistinguishable coming from laptop speakers, but with headphones, the higher sampling rate with lower bitrates sounds better in my opinion. I think that the sampling rate is more important because it helps the soundwaves become smoother, while the bit rate just maintains the quality of each sample.
